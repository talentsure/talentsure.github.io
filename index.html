Technologies


We follow a stringent philosophy for selecting technologies for each project, prioritizing the following criteria:

  • Security: Ensuring that our solutions are robust and protect sensitive data.
  • Scalability: Implementing technologies that can handle growth and changing demands.
  • Hiring: Choosing technologies that have a diverse and expansive talent pool for support and development, making it easy to scale your team.
  • Reliability: Utilizing technologies that have a track record of stability and uptime to ensure a seamless user experience.

We utilize a wide range of technologies, including React, Node.js, Python, Django, GraphQL, Postgres, MySQL, Redis, DynamoDB, React Native, TypeScript, AWS Cloud, GCP Cloud, Docker, and many others. We select technologies based on the project's specific needs and requirements, ensuring that we deliver a tailored solution for each client.
